Welcome to the Evaluation Phase!

Congrats! You have reached the evaluation phase of the ADDIE Model. Please consider these few tips:

  • During the evaluation, consider a post-test survey to see what learning was obtained or not.

  • Provide in the post-survey feedback where participants can input ideas to better the project course.

  • Gather all the data.

  • Was there a change in comparison to the pre-test to post-test, and usability survey? If there was a change, you can revist your project to make modifications.
