Development of Your Project

During the development phase, you are building your project. Please consider these few tips:

  • What tools will you be using to create your project? (Adobe Captivate, H5P, Articulate Storyline, etc.)

  • When completing the development of your project, there needs to be pilot-testing by your chosen learners.

  • Who are your user-testers in order to collect data for effectiveness of your project?

  • Receiving feedback from your user-testers allows you to know what is working effectively and not effectively in your project.
